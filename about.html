About Me

I’m David — a self-taught developer who’s serious about leveling up and building a real career in tech.

My story (simple)

I’m the type of person who likes progress that you can actually measure. When I decide to learn something, I don’t just watch tutorials — I build projects. I like clean design, clear structure, and code that makes sense.

I’m currently focused on front-end fundamentals (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and I’m stepping into more full-stack work. The goal is simple: get really solid, ship consistently, and earn my place in the industry.

How I work

I like step-by-step progress, realistic plans, and finishing what I start. Clean UI, responsive layouts, and good user experience matter to me.

What I’m building

I build portfolio projects that feel real — not just school demos. I’m also interested in bigger ideas like AI-powered apps and translation tools.

What I value

Discipline, consistency, and learning from mistakes. I’d rather improve weekly than pretend I’m perfect.

Skills

  • HTML5 (semantic structure)
  • CSS3 (responsive layout + clean styling)
  • JavaScript (DOM, events, form validation)
  • Git & GitHub (version control + deployment)

Right now, I’m focused on sharpening the basics and building more projects I can proudly show.
